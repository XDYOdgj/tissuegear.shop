Elegance Meets Practicality with the Lahome Moroccan Trellis Area Rug

Bring a touch of timeless beauty and rustic charm to your home with this beautifully crafted Moroccan trellis rug. Its vintage-toned, distress design and soft, felt-like texture create a warm, inviting atmosphere that complements both modern and traditional settings.

Key Features

  • Distinctive faded style with intricate floral patterns
  • Ultra-thin 0.16 inch pile for seamless under furniture placement
  • Non-slip TPE rubber backing for added safety and stability
  • Stain-resistant and non-shedding faux wool material for long-lasting appeal
  • Machine washable for effortless cleaning and maintenance

Where to Use

This versatile rug is perfect for entryways, bathrooms, kitchens, or bedrooms. Its low pile design not only enhances the aesthetic but also prevents pet hair and dander from getting trapped, making it ideal for families with pets or children.

Whether you're looking for Moroccan rugs with a distressed look, vintage area rugs that blend in with your decor, non-slip indoor mats for safety, or 2x4 entryway rugs for style and space efficiency, this rug delivers both beauty and function in one elegant piece. Plus, its washable throw rug properties make it easy to keep clean and fresh for years to come.
