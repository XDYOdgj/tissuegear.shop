GIBZ Non-Slip Rug Pad

Keep your area rugs firmly in place with the GIBZ rug pad. Its non-slip design ensures stability, preventing sliding and bunching for a safer, more comfortable living environment. Crafted with high-density foam, it offers exceptional cushioning underfoot while protecting your floors from scratches and damage.
